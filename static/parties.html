Party Classification Criteria

National Party

  • recognized as a State Party in at least 4 states.
  • Wins 2% of seats in Lok Sabha (11 seats) from at least 3 different states.
  • Polls 6% of valid votes in at least 4 states in Lok Sabha/Assembly elections AND wins 4 Lok Sabha seats.

State Party

  • Polls 6% of valid votes in state elections + wins 2 Assembly seats.
  • Wins 3% of total Assembly seats or 3 seats (whichever is more).
  • Wins 1 Lok Sabha seat for every 25 allotted to the state.
  • Polls 8% of total valid votes in the state without winning seats (Added in 2011).
